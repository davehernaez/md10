Our CEO

William Beday Brandicourt a successful industrialist in Switzerland with his company “Ciresia” bought the Raffinerie Meridionale de Ceresines located in Marseilles, France. In 1923, a refinery specializing in the reprocessing of oil-based products.

Later on moved to Bobigny Paris, after World War II his son Roger Beday took over the company, then Then his son Roland Beday Brandicourt, after serving in the Navy went to work with him as production manager.

During that time the production was transferred to their new factory in Bethisy St. Pierre,Oise north of Paris.

In 1976 the raffinerie was purchased by Mobil Oil Company.

With the recent technology used by France, NASA and AIRBUS, Roland Beday Brandicourt decided to develop MD10, a “nano ceramic industrial lubricants manufacturer” supplying the French territories and the Middle East.

Having retired in Asia he decided to bring the product to the continent, with the aim of reducing the carbon emissions in the region with MD10's revolutionary Eco-Friendly Products.
